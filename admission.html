Sahyadri School is now accepting applications for the 2023–2024 academic year.

The deadline to apply for the 2023–2024 school year is June 15, 2023. Students who submitted their applications by this deadline will be notified on june 25, 2023 as to the status of their acceptance. After this deadline, Sahyadri School will continue accepting new applications on a rolling basis. To apply to Sahyadri School, please start an online application here. Space is limited.

Documents Required.


  1. Birth certificate
  2. Transfer certificate from school last attended.
  3. Bonafide certificate (for a specific academic year)
  4. Sports certificate (for students participating in sports activities)
  5. Academic achievement certificates (such as merit certificates or academic excellence certificates)
  6. 5 passport size photographs.
