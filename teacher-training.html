Workshops and Conferences:
We believe that ongoing professional development is crucial to providing our students with the best possible education, and we offer a variety of workshops and conferences throughout the year to help our teachers stay up-to-date on the latest teaching methods and trends. These events are led by experienced educators and experts in their fields, and cover a wide range of topics, from technology integration to student assessment.



In-Service Training:
In addition to external workshops and conferences, we also offer in-service training to our faculty. These training sessions are led by our own staff, and focus on specific areas of professional development, such as classroom management, curriculum development, and differentiated instruction.



Mentoring and Coaching:
We believe that ongoing support and feedback is essential to the growth and development of our teachers, and we offer mentoring and coaching programs to provide this support. Our experienced mentors and coaches work one-on-one with teachers to help them identify areas of strength and areas for growth, and provide guidance and resources to help them improve their practice.
