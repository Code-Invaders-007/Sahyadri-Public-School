Events

Academic Events: We host a number of academic events throughout the year, such as spelling bees, math competitions, and science fairs. These events are a great opportunity for students to showcase their skills and knowledge in a friendly competition environment.



Cultural Celebrations: Our school is proud to be a diverse community, and we celebrate that diversity with a range of cultural events throughout the year. From Diwali and Holi to Chinese New Year and Eid, our cultural events are a chance for students and families to learn about and celebrate different cultures.



Sports Tournaments: We have a number of sports teams at Sahyadri School, and we host tournaments and games throughout the year. Whether you're a player or a fan, our sports events are always exciting and fun to watch.



Fundraisers: Our school community is dedicated to making a positive impact in the world, and we host a variety of fundraisers throughout the year to support local and global causes. From bake sales to charity runs, our fundraisers are a great way to get involved and make a difference.



Whatever your interests and passions, there's always something going on at Sahyadri School. Check our Events tab regularly to stay up-to-date on all the upcoming events that you won't want to miss!
