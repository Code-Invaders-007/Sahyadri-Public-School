Cultural Arts Festival

A Cultural Arts Festival is a festival that celebrates various forms of art from different cultures. The festival can feature music, dance, painting, and literature, as well as traditional crafts and foods. The event can be organized by a school's art department or a committee made up of students, teachers, and parents.

Language Fair

A Language Fair is an event that highlights the importance of language learning and multiculturalism. This event can showcase the different languages spoken within the school community and the cultures associated with them. It can feature a variety of interactive activities such as language lessons, cultural skits, and games. This event can be organized by a foreign language department or a committee made up of students, teachers, and parents.
