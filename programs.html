Fun and engaging activities for young learners.


At Sahyadri School, we believe that every child deserves a strong start to their educational journey. Our kindergarten program is designed to provide a nurturing and engaging environment that fosters a love of learning and prepares children for success in the years ahead. Through a mix of play-based activities, structured lessons, and social-emotional learning, we help our youngest students build the foundational skills they need to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Building foundational knowledge in core subjects


As students move into the elementary grades, they begin to explore a wider range of academic subjects and extracurricular activities. At Sahyadri School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes language arts, math, science, social studies, and more, all taught by experienced and dedicated teachers who are passionate about helping students succeed. Our students also have the opportunity to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities such as music, art, sports, and clubs, which provide opportunities for personal growth, teamwork, and fun.

Comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for higher education


In the middle school and high school years, our students are challenged to reach their full potential through a rigorous academic program and a wide range of extracurricular activities. At Sahyadri School, we offer a variety of academic pathways, including honors and advanced placement courses, to help students prepare for college and career success. Our extracurricular programs, which include sports teams, clubs, and service organizations, provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, pursue their interests, and make a positive impact on their community.
